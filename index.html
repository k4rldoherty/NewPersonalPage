About Me

Welcome to my corner of the digital realm! I'm Karl Doherty, a dedicated graduate software developer with a passion for crafting innovative solutions through code. With 6 months of professional experience as a full stack developer, I've embarked on an exhilarating journey into the world of technology.

My foundation is built upon a diverse array of programming languages, including Python, C, C#, JavaScript, and TypeScript. These languages are not just tools in my arsenal; they are the means through which I transform ideas into functional, elegant software. My proficiency in this polyglot landscape allows me to navigate the complexities of software development with ease.

Having successfully completed multiple intricate assignments and projects, I have honed my skills in translating abstract concepts into tangible applications. This experience has instilled in me a meticulous approach to problem-solving and a deep appreciation for the intricacies of clean and maintainable code.

Beyond the lines of code, I'm an avid learner, and a fitness enthusiast. I practice some kickboxing and Jiu-Jitsu to keep the mind and body fresh from all those hours sitting at the computer! I'm also an animal lover, and enjoy playing with my dog, Jax.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my journey as a software developer.
